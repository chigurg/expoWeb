'Ça pique' is currently on loan from the private collection of magnet collector Arvo Desloovere. The body of work you see here lends itself to a multitude of interpretations. One speculates that Livia was inspired by a gift she gave to her first love, a cactus. Although art scholars are still strongly debating this, the little plant could be one of the sources of inspiration. Critic Kyarah questions the true inspiration. A source in Fretto's dense circle allegedly gained insight into the search history on Livia's laptop.... Several curious search results were registered on that day, such as: "Easy drawings" and "simple drawing”.
